About Total Wood Flooring

Totalwoodflooring®, based in South West London, is specialised in selling eco-friendly floors including oak and walnut hardwood floorings and luxury Vinyl tiles (LVT) floors. These floors are all produced with the raw material harvested from eco-protected and scientifically monitored forests by qualified companies and sawmills. The beautiful grains of the natural wood plus the elegant design and structures of our products give our consumers the best experience to the wood.
